# Two bets on living longer

I got a little too deep into Claude. It does pull you in, but I have let slide the things that mattered to me. Longevity, meditation, blood tests every six months. Time to come back.

I really do want to live longer. And I am making two bets on it at once.

The first bet is the body. The usual stuff: health, habits, not dying early.

The second bet is digitizing myself. I hand my AI all my thoughts, all my posts, all my voice notes. Bit by bit it learns to both think and write like me.

The more of myself I give it, the more it becomes my copy. Not a bot, a continuation of me. I do not know how many years it will take. But it is the only version of me that can outlive the body.

That is the whole reason I write. First, for myself, like a diary. Second, every note I make fills out that copy of me.

You know the thing about the tree. The best time to plant it was ten years ago. The second best is today. So I plant today. Both trees, the living one and the digital one.

Do you already think about what you will leave behind?
