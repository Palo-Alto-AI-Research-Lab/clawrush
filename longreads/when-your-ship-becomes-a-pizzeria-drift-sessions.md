# When your ship becomes a pizzeria: drift sessions

Lately I keep getting that feeling when you are working on some thing and, well, "started with a bang, ended with a whimper". Or when you set out to build a ship and end up with, I don't know... something like a pizzeria.

As I understand it, this problem is not just mine. It is called drift. Correct me, colleagues, if I am wrong.

What I did about it. I asked my AI to run a self-check: are we drifting, has the focus shifted to something else.

If we are drifting, my AI creates a parallel session and moves the description of the side problem there.

So every problem gets its own session.

And money-wise it is cheaper too. Several narrow tasks are cheaper than one wide one with everything mixed in.

If you have life hacks for what to do when the AI gets carried away solving not the main task but wanders off to the side, share them?
