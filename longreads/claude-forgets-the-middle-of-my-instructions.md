# Claude forgets the middle of my instructions

Colleagues, I need advice. My CLAUDE.md has ballooned. It is the main instruction file for my AI, the one it reads at the start of every session. It is already some three hundred lines.

It seems to me (although no, actually, it does not just seem), when a session lives long, Claude starts forgetting the instructions from this file.

And here is the interesting part. It remembers the beginning of the file, it remembers the end. But the instructions in the middle it forgets. It does not admit this itself, but I have caught it more than once.

I have optimized this file every which way, but the problem still remains.

First idea: put the most important instructions, some twenty of them, at the very top of the file. And let the unimportant stuff live in the middle.

Second idea: sort all three hundred by type. How to sync with other machines. What to do at the start of a session. What to do at the end.

In short, I need advice on how to optimize an instruction file.
