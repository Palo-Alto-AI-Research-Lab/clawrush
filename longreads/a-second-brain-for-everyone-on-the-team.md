# A second brain for everyone on the team

I have already written about how I built myself a second brain out of my own correspondence. Now the next step: we are building one for everyone on the team. The task for my colleagues' AIs: export their entire personal message history, WhatsApp, Telegram, email, and store it safely in their own vault. Naturally, we do not share this information with anyone. Why? So that their agent and their second brain absorb as much data about their owners as possible.

For an AI to write in your voice and answer with your expertise, it needs food: your notes, your outlines, your posts, your correspondence, your answers to questions. A person's style lives in how they write every day. And here is the important part: this is not one shared brain for the whole team. Each person gets their own, personal one, and nobody but the owner looks inside. So gradually everyone on our team ends up with an AI that understands its human and speaks in their voice.
