# Why AI writing reads like a robot

Yes, AI texts annoy me too. When you read a post and you just know a human did not write it. I think about this all the time, including when I make my own texts together with an AI. Too smooth, too correct, not human at all.

What gives a robot away? Probably when all the sentences are too even, unnaturally even, as if written under a ruler, with identical endings. When there is too much water, too many repeats, when the text is so sterile it becomes impossible to read.

In short, the more correct the text, the fewer mistakes in it, the higher the odds a robot wrote it. A normal living person writes nervously. Here long, there short. Here off the plot, there interrupting himself.

How do we try to fix it? Probably the very first thing: we ruthlessly cut the water out of the text.

Second is style. The AI has to write the way I would, not the way an average AI from the big internet writes. For that I feed it a lot of the texts I wrote myself, and teach it to write in my language.

Third is form. My AI is forbidden to write templated texts. It has to change the rhythm, the length, the structure, so that the AI-ness does not jump out at my reader.

I wrote first, second, third, and then I thought: that itself is very AI-like. And I would rather my texts still came out human.
