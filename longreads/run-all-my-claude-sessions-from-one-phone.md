# Running all my Claude sessions from one phone

Here is another thing I have been thinking about. Maybe someone can tell me how best to build it. I have several Claude and Codex instances on several machines. On three machines I have three different accounts. And I need to drive all of them from a single phone. My thinking so far: set up a Telegram account that I write to. Or maybe give every one of my machines its own Telegram account. And on each machine a service that watches all the messages coming into that account or into a Telegram folder. And that way I remotely control the sessions running on each of my computers.

Here is what it looks like. I write to Telegram: give me the list of all my open sessions. The bot gives me the list. I say: drop into that session. It drops in, and I start seeing it, I can write into it and push it further along. And if I feel like it, I switch to another one. I also want search across sessions: find me the session that was about such and such. It searches, I say "drop in," I land inside and see what it was working on. And then I write what I want that session to do next. That way, from Telegram, from one phone, I drive several sessions at once. I picture it as some kind of web app, the way people used to build tap games. Or maybe not a web app at all, just plain text through a Telegram bot. First I think I will do it for Claude, and only then for the rest. I want to look at how Hermes and OpenClaw did it, how their Telegram side works, and then think about how to build it for myself.

So, colleagues, tell me. I would really like to understand what solutions are out there. Has anyone built something like this?
